v4 — What this results screen is, and what it isn't

  • One screen, no story. The 5-step Spotify-Wrapped flow is gone. No tap-to-advance, no story progress bars, no auto-advance. Single page, scannable in two seconds, like Apple Fitness's end-of-workout summary or Apple Stocks's day-end card.
  • Score is restrained, not mega. 80px (capped) instead of 160px. Solid white. No gradient fill, no orange-to-pink. The "/10" sits beside it in --ink-60.
  • Stat list, not glass tally. Apple-Stocks-style hairline-separated rows: Best streak · Total time · Avg per question · Day streak. tabular-nums on every value.
  • Breakdown bars, monochrome. Single white fill on a hairline track. No green-to-blue or amber-to-red gradients. The bar speaks; the color doesn't.
  • Effects stripped: no shimmer animation, no floating confetti loop, no pulsing tap ring, no glow on the score, no kinetic typography reveal. Static, restrained, premium.
  • One accent moment: the "Play again" CTA is the single use of --accent (#ef3340). Everything else is grayscale + a quiet green for the accuracy percentage (functional only — performance signal).
