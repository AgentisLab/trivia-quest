v4 — What this menu is, and what it isn't

  • Layout kept from v3: bento grid (1 hero · 2 medium · 9 standard · 1 spotlight), Live Activity pill below the notch, monoline iconography.
  • Color stripped: the 12-color rainbow is gone. All tiles share --bg-2. Only the Play button and "New" tag carry the single accent (#ef3340).
  • Effects stripped: no animated covers (equalizer, puck pulse, orbiting electrons, etc.), no per-tile gradients, no glow halos, no Daily Challenge banner with social proof.
  • Gamification stripped: the Live pill shows "12 day streak · 238 played" — no XP bar, no level number, no flame icon, no animation.
  • Typography carries the design: SF Pro Rounded for category names + hero, SF Pro Text for body. Weights: 500 / 600 / 700. Sizes: 11 / 13 / 15 / 17 / 22 / 32. Tabular numerals on stats.
