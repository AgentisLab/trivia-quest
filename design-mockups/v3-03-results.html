v3 — Why this is a Wrapped story, not a results page

  • 5-step vertical story: 1) "You played [Category]" hero · 2) Score reveal (shown) · 3) Per-difficulty bars · 4) Streak/XP/Level animation · 5) Share moment + Play Again. Tap-to-advance or auto-advance after 2.5s. Story progress bars at the very top — Snapchat/Instagram lineage.
  • Score is BIG. 160px gradient-clipped numerals (white→gold→orange) with a soft glow. The number is the moment.
  • Kinetic typography: 48px display title, gradient-clipped wordmark, animated shimmer in the background, floating confetti looping endlessly behind the content.
  • Glass tally pill condenses 3 stats into one floating chip (accuracy / streak / XP) — true glass: blur(28px) saturate(180%) + inner highlight + dark base.
  • Step thumbnails on the right column show the other 4 frames of the story so the CEO can see the full arc without me building 5 phones × 2 languages.
  • Tap hint with pulse ring teaches the gesture explicitly. CTA dock at the bottom is always available — never trap the user inside the story.
